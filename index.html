Frequently Asked Questions about Cat Wrangling

Cat Handling, Herding, and Ranching was first established, as a Profession, in South America in the late 16th Century. In 1722 the first Rural Argentina Gaucho Society was formed specifically for the Handling and Breeding of Felines and the trade has only grown since that day. Feline Wrangling is a time honored living for nearly 750,000,000 ranchhands worldwide, that's a lot of rustlers!

A LOT. There is a lot of work on the Ranch, but the time passes quick while you are working. When the work is down you will be showered in cash, more money than oil field workers
